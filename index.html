Research

At Clockwork Systems, I lead the team who is responsible for R&D of Product Suite Cloud Deluxe which commercializes my PhD work of On-Ramp and our patents. It addresses several key technical challenges in network and cloud infra that impede performance and drive-up costs for customer-facing applications and AI/ML workloads. The key products include (read the testimonials by executives of tech giants):

  • Packet Rocket: Detect and mitigate network congestion. Intelligently pace packets to kill network latencies and eliminate packet drops for optimal application performance.
  • Ghost Buffer: Reserve bandwidth headroom to achieve near-minimum network latency for delay-sensitive applications.
  • Bandwidth Slicer: Create programmable bandwidth slices between VMs. Prioritize traffic and ensure critical applications have the necessary bandwidth.

At Stanford, my research topics included edge-based sensing, inferring, learning and controlling in data center networks, e.g. clock synchronization (Huygens), network telemetry and monitoring (SIMON), flow control (On-Ramp) in both on-prem data centers and public clouds, as well as building highly scalable and fault-tolerant distributed systems (e.g. ClockChain, CloudEx) on top of them. Check the following papers and my talks in Stanford Platform Lab annual reviews and retreats for more details.
